\begin{frame}{Why taking this course}
\begin{itemize}
\item To gain a deeper understanding of Chinese political thought and its historical development.
\item To explore the key concepts and ideas that have shaped Chinese political philosophy.
\item To engage with important texts and thinkers in the field.
\item To develop critical thinking skills through the analysis of complex political ideas.
\end{itemize}
\end{frame}
