Course Information
  • Chinese political philosophy.
  • Prequisites: Fundamental of political thoughts and Mandarin at least B2 or above.
  • Time: 13:10 to 15:00, Every Mondays.
  • Venue: Room .
  • Course Description:
    Chinese political philosophy has a rich history that spans thousands of years, encompassing a wide range of ideas and schools of thought. This course will explore the key concepts, thinkers, and texts that have shaped Chinese political philosophy, from ancient times to the present day. We will examine the influence of Confucianism, Daoism, Legalism, and other philosophical traditions on Chinese political thought and practice.
  • Textbook: Chinese political philosophy, written by Kong-Chuan Hsiao.
  • Grading: 100% with final examination.
